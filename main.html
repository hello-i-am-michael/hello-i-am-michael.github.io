TUI T. SUTHERLAND vs
JASON REYNOLDS

Guided Questions:

  1. Is any of your books inspired by real people or events?
  2. What inspired you to have this career?
  3. Will you be continuing this career?

Tui T. Sutherland's Response:

  1. According to an interview on oomscholasticblog.com (specifically talking about her series Wings of Fire), it states “None of the characters are based of people I know, although I was thinking alot about my eldest son as I was writing Clay.” Her idea for the book was to be a book that would be “...[telling] the story from the dragons point of view, instead of seeing them through the eyes of humans...”
  2. According to an interview on publishersweekly.com, it states "I’ve always loved kids’ books, because I feel like children are the most interesting audience and the one you can affect the most. I remember the books I read when I was [young], and I feel like they shaped me and my view of the world, and the kind of person I wanted to be."
  3. Because of numerous sources saying that she is still creating new books, I am assuming that she is still active in the writing industry.

Jason Reynold's Response:

  1. The book The Long Way Down, part of it was inspired from a personal experience by his friend getting murdered when he was 19.
  2. According to theguardian.com, he never wanted to be a writer. In fact he was very old when he read a full book. It was by reading the book that he got insperation to become a writer.
  3. There has been no information that he is still writing today.
